About Julia

Julia Do is an artist working across painting, photography, and memory. Her work explores the intersection of personal and collective histories, often drawing on her experience growing up between cultures.

Artist Statement

My work is an ongoing inquiry into how we remember, misremember, and mythologize the past. Through the language of oil painting and film photography, I reconstruct moments that never fully belonged to me—stories half-told or inherited. The canvas becomes a space where I can slow down and listen to ghosts.
